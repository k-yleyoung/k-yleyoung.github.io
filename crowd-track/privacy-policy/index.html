Crowd Track Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

  • We collect your device's unique identifier (UUID) to create and manage your account
  • This identifier is generated by your device's operating system (iOS)

1.2 Chat Data

  • We store messages you send through our chat feature
  • We maintain records of user bans and their durations

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To moderate chat content and enforce community guidelines
  • To identify and prevent abuse of our services
  • To improve user experience and app functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Chat messages are stored securely on our servers
  • User identifiers are stored in our database to manage chat access and moderation
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data

4. User Bans and Moderation

  • We reserve the right to ban users who violate our terms of service
  • Banned users will be notified of their ban status and unban date
  • Ban decisions are made at our sole discretion

5. Data Retention

  • We retain chat messages as necessary to provide our services
  • User ban records are maintained according to our moderation policies
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting us

6. Third-Party Services

  • We use Firebase services for data storage and authentication
  • Please review Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle data

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kyle@kyleyoung.dev.
